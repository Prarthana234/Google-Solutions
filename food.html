Mission Food Possible

Welcome to Zer0 Hunger

Zer0 Hunger strives to create a sustainable food system by minimizing waste throughout the chain, from preventing unnecessary spoilage to efficiently distributing leftover food to those in need, while also promoting responsible consumption and resource utilization through practices like composting.

The fight against food waste demands a multi-pronged approach. We must prevent food from spoiling in the first place through better planning and storage practices. This includes collaborating with businesses to redistribute surplus or imperfect food to those in need, diverting edible portions from landfills. Additionally, inedible scraps can be recycled through composting or anaerobic digestion, transforming them into valuable resources like fertilizer or biofuel. Ultimately, achieving a sustainable food system requires a collective effort to minimize waste, maximize resource utilization, and ensure food reaches vulnerable communities.

Who are we?

Zer0 Huger is a food waste management system designed to reduce the food wastage and use it in a useful way. We bridge the gap between food waste in abundant societies and food scarcity in vulnerable communities. Our ML model ensures how much food needs to be made at restaurants, hostels, hospitals in order to minimize the waste generated.
